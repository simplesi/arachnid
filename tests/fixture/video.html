Save your energy for the bedroom

07 April 2015

"Insulation - it's sexy stuff" - Barack Obama

Our steamy video featuring Cinderella star Lily James and Dan Rigby was inspired by the US President's excitement over home insulation.

If everyone was this turned on by energy efficiency, our homes would be a lot warmer and cheaper to heat.

Stopping our homes leaking heat is the best way to reduce our energy bills, and to make sure no one has to suffer in a cold, drafty home. What's more, it's one of the fastest and easiest ways to fight dangerous climate change.

But it won't happen by itself.

Friends of the Earth is calling on all political parties to commit to a major, publicly-funded energy efficiency programme which would bring millions of households out of fuel poverty, cut our carbon emissions, and create up to 100,000 green jobs all over the country.

Get MPs from all parties to commit to ending the cold homes crisis.
